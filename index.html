Cosmetics are products or substances used to alter and enhance appearance. Cosmetic are applied are applied externally to the body. Cosmetics have been part of human history for long. Cosmetics products include hair care, skin care, sun care, perfumes, deodorants and makeup. In other word they may be applied to skin, hair, and nail for the purpose of covering, coloring, softening, nourishing, waving, setting, preservation, removal, protection and cleansing. Cosmetics designed for skin care can be used to cleanse, exfoliate and protect the skin, as well as replenishing it, through the use of cleansers, toners, serums, moisturizers, and balms. Cosmetics designed for more general personal care, such as shampoo and body wash, can be used to cleanse the body.
